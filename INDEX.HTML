🏛️ Government Schemes

Schemes That Can Help You

Government programs to support your financial journey.

🏦

Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana

Zero balance bank account with insurance.

  • Zero balance bank account
  • Free RuPay debit card
  • ₹2 lakh accident insurance
  • ₹30,000 life cover
💼

Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana

Small business loans without collateral.

  • Loans ₹50,000 to ₹10 lakh
  • No collateral needed
  • Shishu, Kishore, Tarun categories
👧

Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana

Save for daughter's education with high interest.

  • ~8% interest rate
  • Start with ₹250/year
  • Tax-free returns
🛡️

PM Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana

₹2 lakh life insurance at ₹436/year.

  • ₹2 lakh life cover
  • Only ₹436/year
  • Ages 18-50 eligible
